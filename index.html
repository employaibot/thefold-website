The Problem

Healthcare was built on
the assumption that everyone can stand.

For over 12.2 million wheelchair users — including 250,000 veterans — this assumption has led to a healthcare system that unintentionally excludes them. Critical health data like weight, posture, and hydration is rarely captured at home. The Fold changes that.
